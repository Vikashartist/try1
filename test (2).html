What is The Future of Attendance ?
The future of attendance systems is likely to be shaped by advancements in technology, focusing on efficiency, accuracy, and user experience. Here are some key trends and innovations expected in the future:
Face recognition-based attendance systems offer unparalleled ease of use, where employees, students, or participants simply walk in front of a camera to mark their attendance.
